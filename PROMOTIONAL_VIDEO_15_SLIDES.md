## 🎥 **Production Guidelines**

### **Visual Quality:**
- **Resolution:** Minimum 1080p (Full HD)
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps or 60fps
- **Format:** Vertical (1080x1920) for Instagram/TikTok OR Square (1080x1080) for feed posts
- **Screen Recording:** Use high-quality screen recorder (AZ Screen Recorder, XRecorder)

### **Transitions:**
- **Style:** Smooth, modern (fade, slide, zoom)
- **Duration:** 0.3-0.5 seconds between slides
- **Consistency:** Use same transition style throughout

### **Text Overlays:**
- **Font:** Modern, clean sans-serif (Montserrat, Poppins, Inter)
- **Size:** Large enough for mobile viewing
- **Color:** High contrast with background
- **Animation:** Fade in/out or slide up

### **Music:**
- **Style:** Upbeat, energetic, modern
- **Tempo:** 120-140 BPM
- **Volume:** Background level, don't overpower voiceover
- **Licensing:** Royalty-free (Epidemic Sound, Artlist, YouTube Audio Library)

### **Voiceover:**
- **Tone:** Friendly, enthusiastic, professional
- **Pace:** Clear and moderate (not too fast)
- **Language:** English or Filipino (Taglish works for PH audience)
- **Quality:** Clear audio, no background noise

## 📱 **Screen Recording Tips**

### **Preparation:**
1. **Clean Device:**
   - Clear notifications
   - Full battery or hide battery percentage
   - Set time to 9:41 AM (Apple standard)
   - Airplane mode or full signal bars
   - Hide status bar (if possible)

2. **Test Data:**
   - Use realistic sample data
   - Add professional vehicle photos
   - Create test bookings with proper dates
   - Ensure analytics has meaningful numbers

3. **Navigation:**
   - Practice smooth, deliberate gestures
   - Slow down tap/swipe motions
   - Pause on important screens (3-4 seconds)
   - Show complete user flows

### **Recording Process:**
1. Record each screen/flow separately
2. Record multiple takes (use best one)
3. Keep phone steady (use tripod/mount)
4. Ensure good lighting on screen
5. Record in landscape if showing on laptop/tablet

### **Interactions to Capture:**
- Swiping through vehicle listings
- Tapping on map markers
- Scrolling through vehicle photos
- Selecting dates on calendar
- Typing in search bar (smooth, realistic)
- Tapping buttons with visible feedback
- Notification animations
- Loading states (brief, not too long)

## 🎬 **Editing Workflow**

### **Software Options:**
- **Mobile:** CapCut, InShot, Adobe Premiere Rush
- **Desktop:** Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, DaVinci Resolve

### **Editing Steps:**
1. **Import** all screen recordings
2. **Cut** each clip to exact duration needed
3. **Arrange** in storyboard order (Slides 1-15)
4. **Add transitions** between clips
5. **Insert text overlays** with animations
6. **Add background music** and adjust volume
7. **Record/add voiceover** (or use text-to-speech)
8. **Color correction** for consistency
9. **Add sound effects** (optional: tap sounds, swoosh)
10. **Export** in target format

### **Final Export Settings:**
- **Instagram Reels/Stories:** 1080x1920, 30fps, H.264, high bitrate
- **TikTok:** 1080x1920, 30fps, H.264
- **YouTube Shorts:** 1080x1920, 30-60fps
- **Facebook/Instagram Feed:** 1080x1080, 30fps
